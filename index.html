BusMall is a startup company looking to publish a catalog of assorted high-markup products. These catalogs will be placed in Puget Sound regional transit system buses for passengers to have as a mental escape from the long travel times. BusMall would like you to participate in a market analysis by helping them understand which items to feature in their catalog.

Please click on one product from the three displayed images that you would most likely purchase. Three new images will appear after each selection and will repeat a total of 25 selections. You can participate more than once by simply refreshing the page.
