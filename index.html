Bus Mall

BusMall is a startup company looking to publish a catalog of assorted high-markup products. These catalogs will be placed in Puget Sound regional transit system buses for passengers to have as a mental escape from the long travel times. BusMall would like you to participate in a market analysis by helping them understand which items to feature in their catalog.


Please click on one product from the three displayed images that you would most likely purchase by clicking on the image. After each selection, another three images will reappear. Each time images display please select one, from the three, that would be more inclined to purchase. These images will reappear 25 times to give you an opportunity to make your selections.
